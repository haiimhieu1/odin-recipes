Spring Roll


Description

Spring rolls are a popular Asian appetizer consisting of savory, shredded fillings—typically cabbage, carrots, mushrooms, and sometimes pork or shrimp—wrapped in a thin wheat flour wrapper and deep-fried until golden and crispy. They are usually cylindrical, served hot with dipping sauces, and often symbolize wealth during the Chinese New Year.

Ingredients

  • Rice papers
  • 1 package vermicelli rice noodles
  • Lettuce wrap
  • Cucumber
  • 1 pound small, cooked shrimp
  • 1 bunch of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 pound of pork belly

For the peanut sauce:

  • 3/4 cup sweet chili sauce
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
  • 1/3/cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/3 cup water
